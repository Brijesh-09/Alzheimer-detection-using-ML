About Early Alzheimer's Detection

Early detection of Alzheimer's disease is crucial for several reasons:

  • It allows for timely intervention and treatment, improving patient outcomes.
  • It enables better planning and management of healthcare resources.
  • Early detection empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.

By leveraging machine learning algorithms, we aim to enhance early detection efforts and ultimately improve the quality of life for individuals affected by Alzheimer's disease.
